Ingredients of Leftover Roti Samosa

  • 3-4 leftover chapatis/roti (For wrapping)
  • Maida and water paste (For sticking)
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • 2tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Half tsp grated ginger
  • 1 green chilli, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander power
  • 2-3 boiled potatoes
  • Salt as per taste

How to make Leftover Roti Samosa

  1. let's begain with preparing filling. Just heat a pan and add oil,cumin,seeds,coriander powder,asafoetida,ginger,green chilli,turmeric powder,red chilli powder,coriander leaves and mix them well.
  2. Add roughly chopped boiled potatoes and mix it with spices. Nicely mash the potatoes and keep the filling aside to cool down.
  3. Cut the leftover roties in half
  4. Take one half of roti and apply the maida and water paste at the sides to sick. Press it from the ends and shape it like a cone.
  5. Add the filling in the cone and apply the maida paste to end to seal the samosa.
  6. Fry it until samosas are golden brown and crusty.
  7. Serve them with chutney and chai.
