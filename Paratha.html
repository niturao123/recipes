Aalu Paratha recipe

This is very delicious aalu partha. This is a famous dish of Punjab and whole India.

ingrediance

  • aalu50gm
  • chili20gm
  • flour100gm
  • oil30gm
  • masala5gm

Process to make Aalu Paratha

  1. Boil the potatoes on high flame until they are cooked.
  2. Fry the potatoes in oil until their color turns brown.
  3. We will knead the flour and melt it and then put potates in the flour.
  4. After this make the dough like a roti and boke it for 5 minutes.
  5. After this our yummy paratha is ready.
